Restoring the Soil with Christina "Kat" Cat

Integrity-led business, regenerative soil practices, and the future of food.

Quick Rundown

What you'll hear in this episode

Kat shares how sapropel restores oxygen-deprived lakes while delivering nutrient-dense soil regeneration for farmers.

Integrity, creativity, and honest collaboration shape the way she leads her business and community partnerships.

The conversation explores the link between soil health and gut health, plus her roadmap to retail expansion.
