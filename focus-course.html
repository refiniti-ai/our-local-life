FOCUS on Peak Performance

Free the body, free the mind. A 6-week journey connecting fascia, breath, heart, and clarity for entrepreneurs.

Every week answers: Why does anyone care? And how does this increase peak performance?

Why this course?

Your body is a living feedback system. Thoughts, emotions, and beliefs show up in your physiology, and the other way around. This course decodes that connection so you can release what holds you back and step into focus, clarity, and sustained peak performance.

Curriculum

Six weeks. One question each week.

Week 1

Smile, Laughing & Neuroplasticity

Repatterning through the face and body. How we learn, why we repeat patterns, and Tabata-style smile/laugh practice to connect intention to your neural networks.

Week 2

Fascia, Hips & Spine

How fascia, hips, and spine hold memory and charge. Three stretches/movements to build neural connections and momentum toward clarity and confidence.

Week 3

Cell Salts & Hydration

How salts and water work in the cell. Celtic Sea Salt and spring water on waking and at wind-down. Sweat daily for one week. Signs of dehydration in body and spirit.

Week 4

Breath & Thought Connection

How we breathe shapes how we think. Diaphragmatic breath, challenge reps, and heart-based gratitude breathing to optimize brain and state.

Week 5

Gratitude & Heart Coherence

The heart as the bridge between spirit and body. Intention and how to use it. Combining hydration, fascia/hips/spine release, and heart-based breathing with intention.

Week 6

Systems & Organ Teammates

How sexual, digestive, and other systems store charge and recruit bodily teammates. “Fasting” per system and its effect on the brain and long-term reward.
